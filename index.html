A project for the fans, from the fans

Sonic the Comic, published by Fleetway Editions between 1993 and 2001, was a British combination games magazine and comic book including news, video game reviews, and comics of video games for Sega systems.

These scans are strictly for archival and educational purposes, intended as an element of the transformative work "Sonic the Comic the Podcast" which documents and provides commentary on the entire publication. They are not intended for distribution, circulation, or any other purpose outside of this context.

Under no circumstances are any files in this archive to be used for commercial purposes.

The scans available to download from this site are hosted on the Internet Archive as .cbz files contained within upload zip files. Download speeds are dependent on the archive's server load. The links on this site are direct to the Internet Archive. This site acts as a quick method to link and navigate to all issues.

Privacy and zero cookies

We do not track your usage of the content of this site. This website is completely cookie-free.
